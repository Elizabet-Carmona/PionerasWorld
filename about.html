Meet us

This project was born from the union of 3 girls in a frontend bootcamp dictated by the PionerasDev community in Medellín - Colombia. Which encourages the inclusion of women in the field of web development, this inspired us to meet more girls in the world who are learning to program and give them advice and recommendations to improve. Because among girls we help each other #WomanPower
